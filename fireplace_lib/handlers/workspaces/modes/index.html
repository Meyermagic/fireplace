Collection of different Modes

Modes handle how Views on an Output or Workspace are layouted and behave. They handle positioning, sizing, reacting to View's requests or may choose to ignore them.

They can be used individually as handlers, but are normally indirectly created and managed by workspaces via their matching configurations.
